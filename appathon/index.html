Friday 13th of february 2015

We are glad to announce that Lundalogik Appathon is back again.

This amazing event will take place on the second Friday of February and we are hoping that every office will participate. So pick up your calendar and save the date because this will be fun!

Whats up?

So what is an Appathon? Last spring some of us were introduced to this phenomenon by MHE, SSM and FPE (thanks for that!), but for those of you who couldn't participate last time, here is a short walkthrough.

An Appathon can be described as an event where people from different departments work together in teams to create cool Lime Bootstrap apps that our customers would like to have/buy, or perhaps something that we could use for ourselves!

Who can contribute?

You! An app is as much about the idea behind it as it is about the code. More information about what you can do will come with the rules, but in the meantime let’s just say that this will be an event where everyone can join.

About the event

  • 15:00 – Start, a small presentation
